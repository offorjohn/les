L2PRO Africa Highlights

L2PRO Africa will increase awareness and aid in a better understanding of the IP domain. You will learn how to

  • Protect innovations with patents,
  • Use copyrights to protect software,
  • Develop trademarks,
  • Integrate IP considerations into company business models
  • Obtain value from research and development (R&D) efforts.
  • Enable innovators to access tools to help bring innovations quickly to market.

L2PRO Africa E-Learning IP Training platform

The L2PRO Africa course has 11 modules, which are distributed into two levels: Introduction to Intellectual Property Rights and Filing Process in Africa. Each module explains the concept using interactive media. Every lesson also provides links to additional resources on the subject.
